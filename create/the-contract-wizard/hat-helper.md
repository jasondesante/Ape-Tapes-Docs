# Hat Helper

<figure><img src="https://26wnavfjh6ksl77qri2y5p45a3tv5ma62fv5tmo662fgy6its2pq.arweave.net/16zQVKk_lSX_8Io1jr-dBudesB7Ra9mx3vaKbHkTlp8" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The Contract Wizard has a special companion, and that's his hat!  The Contract Wizard's Hat loves to help people, and is found everywhere around the site where help might be needed.  If you are ever confused, look for the hat!

Just talk to the Contract Wizard and say that you need help and then you'll get some help from the hat!

The hat will be able to answer many questions about The Contract Wizard, the 721J token's functionality and more!
