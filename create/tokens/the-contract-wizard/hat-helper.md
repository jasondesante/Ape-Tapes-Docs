# Hat Helper

## Help Everywhere

<figure><img src="https://26wnavfjh6ksl77qri2y5p45a3tv5ma62fv5tmo662fgy6its2pq.arweave.net/16zQVKk_lSX_8Io1jr-dBudesB7Ra9mx3vaKbHkTlp8" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The Contract Wizard has a special companion, and that's his hat!  The Contract Wizard's Hat loves to help people, and is found everywhere help might be needed.  If you're ever confused, look for the hat and click him!

When you're on The Contract Wizard's page, talk to The Contract Wizard and say that you need help and see what happens!

The hat will be able to answer many questions about The Contract Wizard, the 721J token's functionality and more!

## Look Around!

If there's something you don't understand, look for the tiny hat to help you!

## More Help Needed

If you need more help, it's available. In the settings page, there's a "Toggle hat helper" button. Turning the help on will show hat helper icons everywhere that extra information might help.&#x20;

### You Can Help Too!

Please give your feedback of where you'd like more help, or better written help, and the hat helper will make sure to have more information in the next update.
