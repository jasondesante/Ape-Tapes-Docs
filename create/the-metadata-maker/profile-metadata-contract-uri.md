# Profile Metadata (contract URI)

The Profile Metadata Maker gathers the pictures, description, important links and more for your record label. &#x20;

## Required

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-17 at 12.25.45 AM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The required fields in the Profile Metadata Maker are: &#x20;

* Title/Name
* Image
* Banner Image

The "Required" tab also contains an optional "Description" field (it's recommended you fill that out too).

## Optional

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-17 at 12.25.19 AM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Other optional fields include bonus images, and [social media links](metadata-standards/contract-metadata.md#social-media-links). Use the extra images to make your record label stand out.

## Why ContractURI is Essential

When you look at how NFTs have their own tokenURI, why doesn't the contract itself have it's own piece of metadata? It only makes sense. Why not call it contractURI? This is definitely one of those things that needs to be a part of the NFT standards. It also seems like a bunch of other people have also came to the same conclusion and decided on the same name, contractURI. It makes so much sense, and feels like a natural evolution to do so.

This is why having a Mega Music Metadata Maker with a Profile Maker is important. People could use The Metadata Maker to create metadata for their own projects, they're not being forced to make a contract or tokens or anything. The Metadata Maker is a powerful tool that enhances any platform it's used with.
