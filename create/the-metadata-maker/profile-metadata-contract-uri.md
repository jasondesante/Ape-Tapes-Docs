# Profile Metadata (contract URI)

The Profile Metadata Maker gathers the pictures, description, important links and more for your record label. &#x20;

## Required

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-17 at 12.25.45 AM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The required fields in the Profile Metadata Maker are: &#x20;

* Title/Name
* Image
* Banner Image

The "Required" tab also contains an optional "Description" field (it's recommended you fill that out too).

## Optional

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-17 at 12.25.19 AM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Fill out the extra social media links and add all the extra images you want to make your record label stand out.

Other optional fields for contract metadata include the bonus images array, and [links for all social medias](metadata-standards/contract-metadata.md#social-media-links).

## Why ContractURI is Essential

When you look at how NFTs have their own tokenURI, why doesn't the contract for NFTs have it's own piece of metadata too? It only makes sense. Why not call it contractURI? This is definitely one of those things that needs to be a part of the NFT standarads. It also seems like a bunch of other people have also came to the same conclusion and decided on the same name, contractURI.&#x20;

This is why having a Mega Music Metadata Maker with a Profile Maker is important stuff, and that people could use The Metadata Maker to create metadata for their own projects, they're not being forced to make a contract or tokens or anything, The Metadata Maker is just a powerful tool for artists that enhances any platform it's used in combination with.
