# Staking

Staking is a feature that lets the holder of any token allow anyone to mint copies from it.  Staking is totally optional and is an extra thing that a holder can do to show their support to an artist.  Staking signals that you want more people to mint a particular song, and encourages others to mint a copy from your copy, which gets you referral points.  Referral points might be used for things in the future.

There are 2 states to a token being staked:

Off - No one can mint a copy except the owner of the token.

On - Anyone can mint a copy from the token.
