# Uploading Audio

It's really easy to upload audio to Arweave with The Contract Wizard on ar://listen. Almost too easy. You can do it on your phone or desktop.

### Drag & Drop

Click the box to open a file selector, or drag and drop the file right into the box.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-14 at 1.07.02 AM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Find the audio upload page from the sidebar, browse page, or create page.</p></figcaption></figure>



### Tags & Licenses

You can add quick tags like artist, and genre, and optionally go in depth and add a license and as many audio tags, and custom tags you want.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-14 at 2.17.16 AM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Turbo Credits

The audio uploader and every page that uploads to Arweave use Turbo Credits to upload. &#x20;

You can buy Turbo Credits with crypto or card.  You can easily buy Turbo Credits with your credit card, or Base Eth for example (many networks supported).  You can also sign your uploads with your Eth wallet.  No Arweave wallet required. Once you have a balance of credits, all you need to do afterwards is sign the uploads.

When your balance is enough to upload you should see a green indicator underneat the upload button.

ArDrive Turbo is an open source bundler for Arweave. &#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-14 at 2.20.03 AM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Quick Upload

Once you click upload, all you need to do is sign.  Once the track is uploaded you will see the results page with some options. &#x20;

Those options are:&#x20;

* Play - Listen to the audio, make sure it uploaded properly
* Upload Another Track - Keep uploading files
* Attach Artwork - Immediately upgrade the audio into metadata
* View Your Files - Goes to the uploads page where you can see all the files you've uploaded, view their tags, choose to upgrade any files or metadata, etc.
