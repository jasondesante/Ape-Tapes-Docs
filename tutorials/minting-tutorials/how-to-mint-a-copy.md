# How to Mint a Copy

To mint a copy:

1. Go to the store page or the create page.  They can be found on the sidebar.&#x20;
2. Find a song you want to make a copy of
3. Click on the Copy button &#x20;

<figure><img src="https://hekyv5kv4teebv74zgdcinj3u7bmt6cdegf2q24mzyenrdu42z6q.arweave.net/ORWK9VXkyEDX_MmGJDU7p8LJ-EMhi6hrjM4I2I6c1n0" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4. Choose how you will mint. With ETH or APE
5. Choose if you will mint to your wallet, or another wallet
6. Click Mint
