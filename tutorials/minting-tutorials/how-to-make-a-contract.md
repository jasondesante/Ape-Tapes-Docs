# How to Make a Contract

## First Steps

To create a contract:

1. Click "Create" on the sidebar.

{% embed url="https://dv5nu56xnmqu26fohr5rivkcpijqlsu637ux3yl4sk6v4i6e2cmq.arweave.net/HXrad9drIU14rjx7FFVCehMFyp7f6X3hfJK9XiPE0Jk" %}

2. Click the "Wizard" tab to go to the Contract Wizard page.
3. Talk to the Contract Wizard

{% embed url="https://cvtpdh6rxf7z3s5csmzqjserhypkmfzrpcfun6qui2dydapccb2a.arweave.net/FWbxn9G5f53LopMzBMiRPh6mFzF4i0b6FEaHgYHiEHQ" %}

4. When the Contract Wizard asks if you are here to make a contract, click "Yes".
5. Enter the name, symbol, and price for your new contract. Example: Ass Contract, DN, 0.42069eth

{% embed url="https://m5hcr45dqbc6w5uojcojxvqjyvmixejjopbumgzmpd7pzijldpja.arweave.net/Z04o86OARet2jkicm9YJxViLkSlzw0YbLHj-_KErG9I" %}

6. Choose if you will mint the contract with ETH or APE.
7. Confirm in your wallet then wait for network confirmation
8. Congratulations!  You now have your own contract!



{% embed url="https://n57rslg5uiipoqav7w5cmstodt6jdiwtqboaj4d563wkisdnlxla.arweave.net/b38ZLN2iEPdAFf26JkpuHPyRotOAXATwffbspEhtXdY" %}

## Next Steps

Now that you have your own contract, you can click the "Contract Manager" button to go to the Contract Manager and manage your contract. [the-manager](../../create/the-manager/ "mention")
