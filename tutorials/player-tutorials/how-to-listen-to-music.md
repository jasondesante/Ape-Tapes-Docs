# How to Listen to Music

To listen to music on Ape Tapes:

1. Find some music, by searching something, checking your wallet, or clicking on something while browsing.
2. Each song/token will show up in a long list
3. Click the button on the left side of the row corresponding to the token.  In Alpha 2.0 of Ape Tapes the play button for each song has its tokenId in the button.
