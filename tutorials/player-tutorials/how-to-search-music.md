# How to Search Music

To search music on Ape Tapes:

1. Click the search bar in the top left of the page.
2. Write your search query.  Enter a contract address, wallet address, ENS, or metadata tags.
3. Click enter or search, and watch the results show up.
4. Click the song you want to play.
5. Listen to the song.

Advanced Search:

* Enter “chain: polygon” at the end of your search to enable searching on polygon.&#x20;
* &#x20;"chain: bsc” at the end of your search will change the chain to bsc.
* “ filter: name” will search by name in metadata.
* “ filter: description” will search only the description in metadata
* “ filter: attributes” will search only the attributes in metadata
* These are ways to make search run faster.
