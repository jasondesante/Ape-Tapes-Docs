# How to Search Music

To search music:

1. Click the search bar in the top left of the page.
2. Write your search query.  Enter a contract address, wallet address, or ENS when searching ETH. When searching Arweave, enter any tx\_id for a file of audio, metadata, or playlist.
3. Click enter or search, and watch the results show up.
4. Click the song you want to play.
5. Listen to the song.
