# Trouble Logging In

1. If you don't have a wallet, get one!
2. If you are not signed in, then find a "Log In" button and click it.
3.  **When the sign in window pops up, you'll see a warning that looks scary but isn't.** Metamask is just confused about decentralized sites and throws this warning for any Arweave-hosted app.

    **Here's the deal:** Only the specific gateway `https://contractwizard.arweave.net/` will sign in without the warning because that's the URL the backend expects. Other gateways work perfectly for browsing, but Metamask gets cranky during wallet connection because it can't match the server signature.

    **Bottom line:** Use `contractwizard.arweave.net` for signing in, ignore Metamask's drama, and you're good to go. This isn't a bug, it's just wallets being old-school about the permanent web.

4. It will then ask "Are you sure?" and you have to click the checkmark and then the "Confirm" button. For TWO separate warnings!!

5. After clicking "Confiirm" you will see the "Log In" button has been replaced with your wallet address on the sidebar to show you are signed in.
6. If you didn't sign in fast enough, you will have to try again.
7. Now that you are signed in, you can click "Library" on the sidebar to go to your wallet.
8. Your wallet will load and you will be able to listen to your tokens.
