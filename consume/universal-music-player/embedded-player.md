# Embedded Player

## Embedded Player

You can embed a player into your site easily. The embed link can be to a contract or wallet, and they can include filtering and sorting in the url. &#x20;

The embed player links can even be a serverless playlist which turns the embedded player into a serverless embedded player!

## Generating Embed Link

Click any song and there will be a button to embed the song.&#x20;

You can also take any link to the player and pop it into the Embed Config page in the settings.  This will generate an embed link for you and give you some options.

## Configure the Embed Window

Go to the Settings page, and there will be a button called "Config Embed". &#x20;

The Embed Config page lets you configure the embedded link.  It generates a preview of the embed link right next to the input so you can tweak how things look.
