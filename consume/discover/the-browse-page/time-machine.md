# Time Machine

The Arweave Time Machine is a magical device that lets you scroll back through time and crate dig through old files.

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-12 at 5.18.58 AM.png>)

## How To Activate

Spin the thumbwheels to roll back time. &#x20;

Click scan to see what you found while traveling back through the ages.

Time Machine activated.

## How To Enjoy

Choose from the regular list view or the fun 3d crate digging mode.

Use time machine to browse all files on Arweave, or switch to files uploaded with The Contract Wizard, for a higher probability of tracks with artwork.

Enjoy the art of the track on the cover of the record, or a randomly generated one to keep the vibes. &#x20;
