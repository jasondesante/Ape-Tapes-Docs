# The Browse Page

Browse Page lets you browse more libraries than just The Wizard's Library.  You can also browse Spinamp which is a wonderful index of web3 music that helps organize many platforms into one feed.

The Browse Page lets you view:

* _**Contract Wizard's Library**_ - Browse the list of contracts in The Wizard's Library

#### Spinamp Browse Features:

* _**Recent Songs**_ - The most recently released tracks across many web3 platforms
* _**Featured Playlists**_ - A static list of featured playlists
* _**Trending Playlists**_ - A constantly changing list of the trending playlists
* _**Artists**_ - The full list of artists in the Spinamp Library
