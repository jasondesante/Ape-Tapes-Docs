# Your Wallet

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-12 at 4.55.06 AM.png>)

This page shows all the tokens in your wallet that have audio in them.  In your wallet you can filter and sort all the tokens in any way you want. &#x20;

If you want to save a snapshot of your wallet with particular filters and sorting you can create a playlist of that curated list. &#x20;

Specifically this page is looking for "animation\_url" in the metadata, which results in videos and other things being caught when looking up wallets.  This is why the curation layer is so important to filter out those unwanted tokens.
