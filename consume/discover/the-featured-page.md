# The Featured Page

The Featured Page is currently the home page when you go to Listen.  There are 4 modules in The Featured Page:

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-12 at 3.50.58 AM.png>)

## The Wizard's Library

View all the contracts in The Wizard's Library on the selected network.  Clicking on an image will load that contract's collection.  Works on Ethereum mainnet and Sepolia testnet.

## Recently Uploaded

View the most recently uploaded audio files to Arweave.  These could be raw audio files or metadata.

## Featured Songs

View tracks that are staked to the store.  Every track is staked from a contract in The Wizard's Library.  These are songs that will play if you click them. &#x20;

## Recent Playlists

View the most recent shared playlists that were uploaded through the playlist creator.
