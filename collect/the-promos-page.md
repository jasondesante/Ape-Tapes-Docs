# The Promos Page

The Promo Page shows all the promos your wallet is eligible for.  Every contract in The Wizard's Library (from the chain you're on) could show up in The Promos Page.

### About Promos

Promotional mints can be free, or any price.  They can be open to the holders of any token from a collection or just a specific rarity of a specific song.  This is another way for the artist to be creative.

### Navigating

You can sort through all the contracts that you have eligible promos for. Each individual promo is marked with a new set of Needs/Reward.&#x20;

* Show/Hide: Toggles showing claimed and sold out promos, or just showing eligible promos. By default any claimed promos or sold out promos are hidden, but toggling this will show all those promos too.
* Select a contract: Filter promos to just one contract
* Needs: Shows the requirements of a promo.  What tokens you need from what contract.
* Reward: Shows the token that will be minted for the promo.
* Promo Price: The % discount of the promo.  It can be free, any % discount, normal mint price or more.  Example below shows free.
* Mint Token Button: Click to mint the promo token.  If it is claimed it will say "claimed", below the yellow button is claimed.
