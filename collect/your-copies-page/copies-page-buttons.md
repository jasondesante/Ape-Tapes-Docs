## Staking Button

The middle button of the three is the "Staking" button.  It's icon looks like the Contract Wizard without a hat.  When the token isn't staked, it looks like this. &#x20;

<figure><img src="https://2khmmunqiol6jcwhtiaac5657qip5xggbv4mh6bhdhbok3ipeqcq.arweave.net/0o7GUbBDl-SKx5oAAXfd_BD-3MYNeMP4JxnC5W0PJAU" alt=""><figcaption><p>A token that isn't being staked</p></figcaption></figure>

When a token is staked, it lights up.  This lets you quickly see which tokens in your collection are being staked and which are not.

<figure><img src="https://72hi7iqxz6ygmbmdb2dvutrclz3ehji2zoc2wnzroa5aha4ug7ta.arweave.net/_o6PohfPsGYFgw6HWk4iXnZDpRrLhas3MXA6A4OUN-Y" alt=""><figcaption><p>A token that is being staked</p></figcaption></figure>

When you click the staking button a window pops up that shows something like this:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-07-09 at 11.01.07 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

In this window you can see the staking status of the token.&#x20;

* Red - Staking Disabled
* Green - Staking Enabled
* Orange - Sold Out

To stake or unstake a song, simply click the status button and confirm. When a token is staked it will show up in The Store Page and be available to mint by anyone. If someone mints a copy from your token, it will gain a referral point for you.

### Referral Points

The amount of "Referral Points" is a number counting how many times a copy has been made from a particular token.  It counts how many times a token has been used to make other copies. Maybe it has been gaining "experience points" or "leveling up".  This is to encourage and recognize who your biggest supporters are.  More can be done about referral points in the future. &#x20;

## Sold Out

Once a token is sold out, it's copy button disappears because you aren't able to make copies of that song anymore. The staking button changes color to show the minting stage has completed!
