# Your Copies Page

## Your Personal Collection

This is where you see the tokens in your wallet from the Wizard's Library.  On this page, you'll see every contract in The Wizard's Library that you own tokens from.

All tokens on The Copies Page can be copied and staked.  You can also view referral points and other info about your tokens.

### View All Contracts

You can view The Wizard's Library on this page. Every contract that you own contracts from.  Toggle through them at the top with the +/- buttons, or use the search functionality to pick the collection you're looking for.
