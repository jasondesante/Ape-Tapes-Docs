# Target Audiences

## Artists

Anyone who makes a digital creation would need to create digital originals to cryptographically prove they made it.  The Contract Wizard lets you do that with the ability to create copies from the original.  Even if you don't want to sell copies, in a future where everything is infinite, it is important to make digital originals of your art work.

## Collectors

Anyone who wants to collect digital collectibles of art can mint a copy from a master or copy.  Serious collectors can collect full sets by completing an album of songs, collect only top rarities, or by leveling up their favorite song with referral points and staking.

## Listeners

Anyone who wants to listen to any music that was released as a token can search it up and find the contract or wallet. &#x20;

You can share playlists that can be listened to without a server backend.&#x20;

You can listen to your own wallet, or anyone else's wallet if you know their address.  Same with and contract address.  If you know an artist's name, song, album, or genre you can search that too.

## Investors

Anyone who wants to seriously invest in an artist can seek out high level rarity copies. More rare copies could have more benefits, depending on how the artist decides to do it, including rights or licenses to the song.  The original token will always be the top rarity, 1/1 of the song with its own unique metadata that all copies were derived from.
