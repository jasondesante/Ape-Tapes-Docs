# Creating A Playlist

**Creating A Playlist**

Curating is the act of sorting through all the music to create beautiful organized playlists that can be shared. Taking under documented NFTs and files and enhancing their metadata and discoverability.

**The Curator's Mission**

Listeners have a crucial job: when you discover music that moves you, act on that love. Favorite tracks, create playlists, tag them properly, and upload them. Your genuine passion for great music only spreads forward when you harness it into curation.

This creates a natural feedback loop. Your curated playlists help others discover the music you love, which leads to more curation, more discovery, more spread of quality music. Decentralized curation works when passionate listeners become active curators.

**Easy To Make**

Anyone can create a playlist when browsing and listening to music.

**Private Until Publish**

Playlists are private until you publish them. When you publish a playlist, it gets uploaded to Arweave and becomes playable across the Permaweb.
