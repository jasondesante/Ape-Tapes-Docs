# Adding Songs

### Open Playlist Menu

You can add songs to a playlist with either "+" buttons.  Once you click the "+" or "Add to Playlist" button, you will see the list of playlists you have created. &#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-12 at 10.54.44 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Use the "+" buttons to add one or multiple tracks to a playlist</p></figcaption></figure>

### Select Playlist

Now click the green "+" to add the track(s) to the selected playlist.
