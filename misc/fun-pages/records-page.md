# Records Page

Tracks site activity with tabs: general, songs, insights, achievements. Includes charts for top interactions.&#x20;

The Records Page has lots of fun stats to see your activity on the site over time.

General Stats has over 80 stats across 10 different categories.

View your listening activity on the Song Stats page.

### Insights

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-07-31 at 3.32.04 AM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The Insights Page shows you different leaderboards related to your activity around different subjects.  Most listened to track, most searched query, most common genre.  You can view the insights in a chart or list.
